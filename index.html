WHAT IS LGBTQ+?

LGBTQ+ is an initialism that stands for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer. In use since the 1990s, the initialism, as well as some of its common variants fucntions as an umbrella term for sexuality and gender.

LGBTQ+ CAMPAIGNS IN 2022

Lego: The A-Z of awesome

This campaign will take the form of an alphabet out of Lego bricks that teach vocabulary, educate, and help to build acceptance of the LGBTQ+ community through play.

Mcdonald's: "No Judegment" Campaign

This ad shows the players from LGBTQ+ community league enjoying their meal after a kickball game and also saying "Our friendship may have started on the field, but this is our happy place."

H&M: My chosen family: Pride Month 2022 at H&M

This campaign focuses on the concept of non-biological families, which play an essential role for many people in the LGBTQ+ community.
